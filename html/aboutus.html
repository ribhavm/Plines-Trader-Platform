About us

We are an upcoming trade services providing website that aims to link traders such as plumbers, maids, electricians, painters and masons to their prospective client in India. Created by students of IIT Jodhpur, Our aim is to connect the trader to the customer in a way that is mutually beneficial.

Once the work is complete, homeowners leacve a review of the tradesperson- and its this feedback that gives other homeowners peace of mind that they have found someone reliable.

Each tradesperson has a profile page where their reviews are listed, and only homeowners who have found their tradesperson through our site are allowed to leave a review.

For homeowners, posting a job is free. Our tradespeople pay a low subscription fee along with a small cost to qoute for each job.

Our Vision

To connect good homeowners with good tradespeople to deliver better homes, better work and better lives.

We want the excitement and potential of home improvement to be the only thing on people’s minds. Finding a reliable tradesperson should be easy, just the first step in a satisfying process of making the most of your home. Everything we do is shaped towards that feeling.

We work solidly to prevent rogues on our site, and we put the best tradespeople in the spotlight – the ones who are reliable, skilled and trustworthy.

We want tradespeople to have the confidence to grow their business and take control of their workload. Whatever their ambitions, whether it’s to fill a few gaps in the diary, or if they want enough work to take on new staff and hire apprentices – we’re there for them.
