  • Basics

  • Is PLINES free of cost?

    While customers only have to pay the trader’s job charges and no costs directly to the website, any trader listed on our website has to pay a nominal listing subscription fee monthly.

  • Is an account required to access these services?

    Yes our tradespersons are thoroughly verified workers who have their own accounts on our websites whereby they can list the services they provide. Further, customers are also required to create an account to avail these services. An account can be created via email account, phone number or linkedIN account as well. All personal data and online information stored by us will not be used for any mischievous purposes or shared with any third party.

  • How will PLINES handle covid related complications?

    Workers will follow all covid related precautions and SOPs as advised by the Government of India. This includes wearing of masks, face shields and regular sanitation of hands. Other precautions include downloading Arogya Setu app and regular temperature checks.

  • How does the website protect user data?

    Our website will follow SSL secure socket shell protocol, which will allow log in and storing of username and password securely allowing to evaluate and authenticate sign-in data while the user is logging in temporarily.

  • Job

  • How do I post a job?

    Going to the ‘new job’ portal you can enter the kind of tradesperson you are looking for. If the available tradespersons aren’t suiting your requirements you can choose to post a new job and mention the job you want done, in which case this post can then attract bids from the registered tradespersons.

  • How should I choose a tradesman?

    Detailed descriptions, ratings and credentials will be available on the portal on the basis of which you can make your decision.

  • How do I rate a particular job?

    In the section where your job was accepted you can ‘add rating’ and give a rating out of 10 in addition to a text feedback of not more than 150 words.
