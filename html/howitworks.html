How Our Service Works

1. Create a job – it’s free

Whatever you need doing around the house, we can connect you with the right tradesperson. With over 10 specialist trades, covering the whole of the country, every job’s covered. Just give us as much detail as possible so our tradespeople can get you an accurate quote.

3. Check ratings

You’ll get a link to each tradesperson’s profile page where you can see their ratings and reviews. Only homeowners who have found their tradesperson through our site can leave ratings, so they’re based on genuine experiences.

4. Pick a tradesperson

Go through our homeowner checklist, which explains how to review a tradesperson’s qualifications, reputation and agree the work.

5. Job done

After the work is complete, return to the site and rate your tradesperson based on quality of their work, reliability and value for money.

For the Tradepersons

At the log in / sign up portal registered tradespersons can log in to their premium accounts where they can view their past trades history, feedback and average ratings, income earned, commissions and subscriptions fee due, new jobs available, tradesperson job seeking offers uploaded etc.
